**Category:** edge-case
**Description:** DuckDB prints `-- Loading resources from /home/hugh/.duckdbrc` to stdout. Arc captures stdout/stderr separately, so this appears in step output but does not affect correctness.
**Recommendation:** No action needed — this is DuckDB CLI behaviour.
